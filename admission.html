ADMISSION OVERVIEW

Admission for STD XI is based upon the X STD board marks. Criteria is fixed for the three main streams of study offered.

  • Science with Computer Science
  • Science with Biology
  • Commerce with Business Maths
  • Commerce with Computer Science
